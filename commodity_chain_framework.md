## Peace Deal Playbook — THE CRITICAL ASYMMETRY

### What Unwinds FAST (24 hours)
- Oil price: -15-20% immediately
- Tanker rates: normalisation begins
- War risk insurance: repriced within days
- Container rates: Cape routing premium disappears
- **Heating oil / jet fuel: drops immediately**

### What Unwinds SLOW (weeks)
- Naphtha prices: production restarts 2-4 weeks
- LPG flows: 1-3 weeks
- Pharmaceutical API costs: 4-8 weeks
- Container rate normalisation: 2-4 weeks

### What DOES NOT UNWIND — LOCKED IN
**2026 spring planting shortfall.** Crops are in the ground. Harvest volumes for Q3/Q4 are determined by what was planted. Even if fertiliser becomes freely available tomorrow — you cannot replant a missed spring season.

**The Queensland sugar lesson applied:** The damage is done. The market hasn't priced the magnitude.

### Trading Implication
**On a peace deal announcement:**
- Market prices FULL resolution → EUR bid across the board
- **Correct analysis:** PARTIAL resolution — energy reverses, food does NOT
- **The trade:** Fade the initial full-EUR-bid reaction. Food CPI persistence keeps ECB trapped even after oil collapses
- **Timeline:** Initial EUR bid fades within 1-2 weeks as food CPI data continues deteriorating

## Chain Detail

### TIER 1 — Active Trading Relevance

**Chain 1: Fertiliser / Food CPI**
Gulf gas disrupted → TTF +23% → EU fertiliser plants curtailed → Urea +50% → Spring planting shortfall → Q3/Q4 harvest shortfall → Food CPI spike → ECB trapped.
- Gulf producers = 44% of globally traded sulfur
- Edge diminishing (Reuters confirmed May 8) but timing edge on Q3 harvest magnitude remains
- LOCKED IN regardless of peace deal

**Chain 3: Naphtha / Plastics**
Gulf naphtha = 35-40% of global ethylene → EU crackers paying premium → EU cannot quickly substitute (specification lock-in) → BASF announcing surcharges → PPI → CPI in 8-12 weeks.
- European chemicals = 15-20% of EU manufacturing output
- BASF, INEOS, LyondellBasell earnings calls = confirmation signal
- Most underappreciated chain currently

**Chain 8: Cape Rerouting**
+10-14 days transit → +20-25% fuel cost/voyage → container rates +40-60% → goods inflation re-accelerates.
- Currently 25-35% of 2021-22 peak — significant but not crisis-level
- Market adapting over 6-12 months (new vessels ordered for Cape routing)
- Adds ~0.3-0.5% to goods CPI over 6 months

### TIER 2 — High Edge, Building

**Chain 7: Pharmaceutical API**
Gulf naphtha disruption → Indian chemical feedstock costs rise → API production costs → generic drug prices +5-8% → healthcare CPI +0.2-0.3% → cannot strip from core.
- 15-20% of India's pharma precursors transit Gulf or use Gulf-derived feedstock
- This is the next fertiliser story — we are early
- Watch: Teva, Viatris, Sun Pharma earnings calls

**Chain 9 (NEW): Jet Fuel / Aviation**
Gulf jet fuel disrupted → 60-80% premium → airline fuel 25-30% of costs → European airfare CPI → SERVICES inflation (stickier than goods).
- Ryanair, EasyJet, Lufthansa all affected
- Services inflation is what ECB watches most closely
- Already in airline costs, CPI impact in 4-8 weeks

**Chain 2: Sulfur / Phosphate**
Less Gulf crude refined → less sulfur byproduct → phosphate production hit → compounds urea story.
- Gulf = 44% of globally traded sulfur — not marginal
- Best read as Chain 1 amplifier, not standalone
- Moroccan phosphate output = leading indicator

### TIER 3 — Background / Structural

**Chain 10 (NEW): Insurance Cascade** — Lloyd's war risk +800-1200%. Invisible inflation tax on all Gulf-transiting cargo. Diffuse but real.

**Chain 11 (NEW): VLCC Tanker Feedback** — $450K/day rates → $3-4/bbl transport premium → dollar-denominated → structural USD demand. Partially offsets safe haven unwind.

**Chain 5: Copper Smelting** — Weaker than assumed. Most smelters self-sufficient in sulfuric acid. AUD story more than EUR story.

### MONITOR — Wrong Timeframe or Wrong Pair

**Chain 6: Nuclear Renaissance** — Policy shift real (Germany reversing phase-out, uranium >$100/lb) but 3-5 year build. EUR positive long-term. Track Euratom procurement contracts.

**Chain 4: LPG / Asian Petrochems** — Real mechanism, too many transmission steps to EUR/USD. JPY/KRW story.
