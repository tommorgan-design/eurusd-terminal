⚡ QUICK RULES

  • 📏Measure Asian range at London open (8-hour lookback). Range must fall within pair's filter band to qualify.
  • 📅Wednesday and Thursday only. Both pairs share mid-week directional uncertainty — that's the bracket edge.
  • 🎯Place BOTH orders (buy above + sell below) with entry offset. When one triggers, the other is your stop.
  • ❌If neither qualifies — no trade. The filter IS the edge. Forcing a bracket outside the sweet spot = negative expectancy.
  • 🔄Cancel opposite order manually when one side fires (no OCO on IG). IB: use native OCO.
  • 📊Both pairs can fire on the same day if both qualify. Different range profiles = genuine diversification.
  • ⏱️GBP triggers ~1 hour later than EUR. Monitor accordingly.
  • 🔁Monthly retest: Re-run engine first Sunday of each month. Parameters are hypotheses, not laws.
